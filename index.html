Purpose of the System

The Barangay ACLC Online Complaint System was developed to modernize the process of filing, tracking, and resolving community complaints and blotter records.

This system aims to make barangay services more accessible to all residents by providing a convenient online platform available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

  • Digitize all complaint and blotter records for easy retrieval
  • Allow residents to file complaints without visiting the barangay hall
  • Provide real-time updates on case status and resolution
  • Generate official printed reports for legal proceedings
  • Manage user accounts with role-based access control
  • Maintain a transparent and accountable process for all residents
